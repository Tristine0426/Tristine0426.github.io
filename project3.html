    • GREENSKY RESPONSIVE TIMELINE OVERVIEW

    This is one of the projects I worked on during my internship at GreenSky, LLC. I had a chance to work with a SharePoint developer to help with the redesign of their intranet site. I was tasked to create a responsive timeline for their "History" page giving the limitations of SharePoint. The project was designed using the company's style guide to enforced the ADA compliance guideline. It is currently still under production.


      Goal

    • Use ADA compliance color palette
    • Create a responsive timeline that is compatible with mobile, tablet, and desktop.

      Constraint

    • Must work around Sharepoint's limited design customizations.

    PROCESS

      1. Discovery & Research

    • Work with product owner to envision the "ideal" result
    • Work with lead designer for verifications and design discussions

      2. Design

    • Sketches
    • Present to developer

      3. Prototype

    • Hi-Fidelity mockups

      4. Feedbacks & Iterations

    • Present prototypes in design meetings
    • Iterate
    • repeat for feedback and iterate
