    • easeCommute

    easeCommute is a mobile app that gives business travelers the ability to select hotels based on the commute from their office in that city. Whether the user is walking or driving in traffic, easeCommute aims to help users get there faster. It allows travelers to view reviews from Google, insight from their colleagues, planning where to eat, and tracking each expense all in one.
