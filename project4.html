    • GREENSKY TEMPLATE EMAIL OVERVIEW

    This is an email template created during my internship that trains home improvement merchants on how to reach out to their customers. I was giving a list of contents for the email template and was tasked to create a responsive layout that would highlight important data for merchants to reach out to their customers. The email template includes the customer's contact information, services they are searching for, and a sample script for merchants to reach out to their potential customers.
