    This is a take-home assignment


    TASK: Design a mobile interface that gives business travelers the ability to select hotels based on the commute from their office in that city.


    Duration: 1 week


    OVERVIEW

    Problem Statement: Even though employees are required to book hotels or travel expenses throught their company's portal, they still have to use multiple platforms to help them decide which hotel to stay at. Typically, business travelers will input their hotel preferences (location, distance, dates, etc.), then they will see a search results list, and narrow down their interested hotels. At this point, users will open another window to look up reviews, use Google Maps to calculate commute time based on amenities, and restaurants nearby.

    Rooms To Go Mobile Navigation Process

    Most companies with multiple locations around the country allow their employees to travel to different office locations. It is often required. Business travelers have to deal with disruptions in their routines, dealing with the new environment. Specifically, finding a hotel with a short commute time would tremendously help him/her avoid traffic or anything unexpected in a new city.


    Duration: 1 week


    TASK: Rooms To Go currently just list all of the different categories and sub-categories on their mobile navigation (Hamburger menu). Take Furniture > Living Room & Living Room Pieces section and provide an enhanced merchandising experience for this portion of the navigation. The enhancement should provide a better way to promote Living Rooms and Living Room pieces, along with their corresponding sub-categories.
