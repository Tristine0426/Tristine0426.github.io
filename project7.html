    • GREENSKY'S CUSTUMER SERVICE DASHBOARD

    This is a desktop platform developed for the customer service teams to look up a customer's loan application and customer's personal information. It mainly serves customers who are seeking home improvement project loans and healthcare financing options. This project started before I came to GreenSky, but was halted due to other priorities, then picked up again during my internship. As a result, I was given the business requirements through a Jira ticket and tasked to pick up where it was left off.


      Goal

    • Create a search section that is easy to understand with the given criterias.
    • Create an option for the customer service team to view the customer's application plan in details.

    PROCESS

      1. Discovery & Research

    • Dissect requirements through Jira ticket
    • Work with lead designer for verifications and design discussions

      2. Design

    • Sketches
    • Analyze old wireframes

      3. Prototype

    • Hi-Fidelity mockups

      4. Feedbacks & Iterations

    • Present prototypes in design meetings
    • Iterate
    • repeat for feedback and iterate

    OUTCOME

    Unfortunately, this project was not able to launched. It was halted in anticipation for an upcoming product line. However, below are some results of the process.
